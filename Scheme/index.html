Agriculture and Farmers' Welfare Schemes in India

Central Sector Schemes:

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN) +

Purpose: Provide financial support to landholding farmers.

Achievements: ₹2.81 lakh crores transferred to over 11 crore farmers.

Benefits: Helps in supplementing the financial needs of the farmers in procuring various inputs to ensure proper crop health and appropriate yields.

Learn more about PM-KISAN
Pradhan Mantri Kisan Maandhan Yojana (PM-KMY) +

Purpose: Provide pension to small and marginal farmers.

Achievements: 23.38 lakh farmers enrolled.

Benefits: Ensures social security to small and marginal farmers by providing a minimum fixed pension of ₹3000 per month on attaining the age of 60 years.

Learn more about PM-KMY
Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) +

Purpose: Provide affordable crop insurance.

Achievements: 5549.40 lakh farmer applications insured; ₹150589.10 crore paid in claims.

Benefits: Provides coverage and financial support to farmers in the event of crop failure due to natural calamities, pests, and diseases.

Learn more about PMFBY
Modified Interest Subvention Scheme (MISS) +

Purpose: Provide concessional short-term agri-loans

Achievements: 465.42 lakh new KCC applications sanctioned with a credit limit of ₹5,69,974 crore.

Benefits: Enables farmers to get access to affordable credit for agricultural activities..

Learn more about MISS
Agriculture Infrastructure Fund (AIF) +

Purpose: Address infrastructure gaps and mobilize investment in agriculture.

Achievements: ₹33.209 crores sanctioned for 44,912 projects.

Benefits: Enhances the farm-gate infrastructure for farmers to improve their income and productivity.

Learn more about AIF
Formation & Promotion of 10,000 FPOs +

Purpose: Support formation of Farmer Producer Organizations.

Achievements: 7,774 FPOs registered.

Benefits:Enables farmers to gain better bargaining power, access to markets, and improved economies of scale..

Learn more about FPOs
National Beekeeping and Honey Mission (NBHM) +

Purpose: Promote and develop scientific beekeeping.

Achievements: 23 lakh bee colonies registered; 88 honey FPOs registered.

Benefits: Enhances farmers' income through beekeeping, which also helps in pollination and improving agricultural productivity.

Learn more about NBHM
Market Intervention Scheme and Price Support Scheme (MIS-PSS) +

Purpose: Protect growers from distress sales during bumper crop seasons.

Achievements: 23.38 lakh farmers enrolled.

Benefits: Provides a safety net to farmers by ensuring they receive fair prices for their produce during surplus production periods.

Learn more about MIS-PSS
Namo Drone Didi +

Purpose: Provide drones to Women SHGs for rental services in agriculture.

Achievements: Scheme recently approved with an outlay of ₹1261 Crores

Benefits:Empowers women in agriculture by providing them access to advanced technology for farming activities.

Learn more about Namo Drone Didi

Centrally Sponsored Schemes:

Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY) +

Purpose:Create pre & post-harvest infrastructure.

Achievements: 1524 start-ups funded with ₹106.25 crore grants.

Benefits: Enhances agricultural productivity and ensures better price realization for farmers through improved infrastructure..

Learn more about RKVY
Soil Health Card (SHC) +

Purpose: Provide soil health information and recommendations.

Achievements: Nationwide soil fertility map development.

Benefits: Helps farmers make informed decisions about soil management practices, leading to improved crop yields and sustainability.

Learn more about SHC
Rainfed Area Development (RAD) +

Purpose: Promote Integrated Farming System.

Achievements: ₹1673.58 crores released; 7.13 lakh hectares covered.

Benefits: Enhances the productivity and resilience of rainfed agriculture through integrated approaches.

Learn more about RAD
Per Drop More Crop (PDMC) +

Purpose: Increase water use efficiency through Micro Irrigation.

Achievements: 78 lakh hectares covered under Micro Irrigation.

Benefits: Conserves water and improves crop productivity by providing efficient irrigation techniques.

Learn more about PDMC
Micro Irrigation Fund (MIF) +

Purpose: Expand coverage of Micro Irrigation.

Achievements: ₹4710.96 crore projects approved; ₹2812.24 crore disbursed.

Benefits: Provides financial assistance to farmers for adopting micro-irrigation systems, leading to water conservation and increased crop yields.

Learn more about MIF
Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY) +

Purpose: Promote organic farming.

Achievements:Assistance provided for organic practices.

Benefits: Encourages sustainable agricultural practices, reduces chemical usage, and promotes environmental conservation.

Learn more about PKVY
Sub-Mission on Agriculture Mechanization (SMAM) +

Purpose: Increase reach of farm mechanization.

Achievements: ₹6748.78 Crore released; 15,75,719 machinery distributed.

Benefits: Improves efficiency and productivity in agriculture, reduces drudgery, and enhances farm income.

Learn more about SMAM
Crop Residue Management +

Purpose: Promote in-situ management of crop residue.

Achievements: ₹3333.17 crore released; 2,95,845 CRM machinery distributed.

Benefits: Mitigates air pollution, improves soil health, and promotes sustainable agricultural practices.

Learn more about CRM
Agro-forestry +

Purpose: Promote planting of trees on farmlands.

Achievements: Quality Planting Materials (QPM) certification provided.

Benefits: Enhances land productivity, provides additional income streams, and promotes environmental sustainability.

Learn more about Agro-forestry
National Food Security Mission (NFSM) +

Purpose: Increase production of key crops.

Achievements: Millet missions in 13 states; 500 start-ups and 350 FPOs operational.

Benefits: Ensures food security, improves crop diversity, and enhances farmers' income.

Learn more about NFSM
Sub-Mission on Seed and Planting Material (SMSP) +

Purpose: Support seed production and supply.

Achievements: Seed Authentication, Traceability & Holistic Inventory (SATHI) portal launched.

Benefits: Ensures availability of quality seeds, improves crop yield, and promotes crop diversity..

Learn more about SMSP
National Mission on Edible Oils (NMEO)-Oil Palm +

Purpose: Promote oil palm cultivation.

Achievements: Additional area of 6.5 lakh ha under Oil Palm plantation targeted.p>

Benefits: Reduces dependency on edible oil imports, enhances oilseed production, and increases farmers' income.

Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH) +

Purpose: Promote holistic growth of the horticulture sector..

Achievements: 12.95 lakh ha additional area covered; 872 nurseries established.

Benefits: Boosts horticultural production, enhances nutritional security, and provides employment opportunities.

Learn more about MIDH
National Bamboo Mission (NBM) +

Purpose: Develop the bamboo sector value chain.

Achievements: 46000 ha bamboo plantations established; 81 primary processing units established.

Benefits: Promotes sustainable bamboo cultivation, creates employment opportunities, and fosters economic growth

Learn more about NBM
Integrated Scheme for Agriculture Marketing (ISAM) +

Purpose: Improve market structures and access to market information.

Achievements: 1389 mandis integrated to e-NAM platform.

Benefits: Enhances market efficiency, reduces intermediaries, and ensures better prices for farmers' produce.

Learn more about ISAM
Mission Organic Value Chain Development for North Eastern Region +

Purpose: Develop organic production clusters.

Achievements: 379 FPO/FPCs created covering 189039 farmers.

Benefits: Helps in supplementing the financial needs of the farmers in procuring various inputs to ensure proper crop health and appropriate yields.
